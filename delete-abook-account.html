Delete abook Account

To request deletion of your abook account and associated data, please email info@ablest.me from the email address used to create your account.

We will process your deletion request within 30 days.

What happens when you delete your account?

  • Your account will be permanently deleted
  • All associated data will be removed from our systems
  • This action cannot be undone

Note: Please ensure you send the deletion request from the email address registered with your abook account to verify your identity.
